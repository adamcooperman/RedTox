About RedTox

What is RedTox?

RedTox is a Reddit thread toxicity scanner that helps users analyze and filter potentially harmful content from Reddit discussions. It uses advanced AI text moderation to identify and warn about toxic language, allowing you to browse Reddit threads with awareness of potentially offensive content.

How It Works

RedTox scans Reddit threads and analyzes each comment for toxicity using the Friendly_Text_Moderation API.

1. Thread Analysis

Paste a Reddit thread URL and RedTox will retrieve all comments from that discussion.

2. Content Scanning

Each comment is analyzed by our AI to detect potential toxicity across multiple categories.

3. Safe Browsing

View the thread with toxic content warnings and adjust your toxicity threshold as needed.

Toxicity Detection

RedTox identifies various types of harmful content, including:

  • Hate Speech - Content targeting groups based on protected attributes
  • Harassment - Bullying or intimidating language
  • Self-Harm - Content promoting self-harm or suicide
  • Sexual Content - Explicit sexual content
  • Violence - Content promoting or glorifying violence
  • Profanity - Offensive or inappropriate language
  • Threatening Language - Direct threats to individuals or groups
  • Misinformation - Deliberately misleading content
Disclaimer: RedTox is a tool designed to help identify potentially harmful content, but it is not perfect. The AI may miss some toxic content or falsely flag non-toxic content. Always use your judgment when browsing Reddit. This tool is not affiliated with Reddit, Inc.
